V i c t i m s

When anyone thinks about abuse, they think of physical or sexual, they never think about verbal. Verbal abuse is harder to recognize, which means it is harder for victims to get help. However, verbal abuse in and of itself can be deadly. This website is designed to give these victims those resources to reach out and realize that they are not alone.
